# NextCRM Product Mission

## Pitch

NextCRM is an **AI-first, enterprise-grade, open-source CRM platform** built on Next.js that helps businesses of all sizes manage customer relationships, automate sales processes, and leverage AI-powered insights—all while maintaining complete data sovereignty through self-hosting.

Unlike proprietary CRM solutions that lock you into expensive subscriptions and third-party data control, NextCRM provides enterprise-quality features with the freedom of open-source software and the power of modern AI.

## Vision

To become the **best Next.js open-source self-hosted CRM alternative** by combining:
- **AI-First Architecture**: Maximum utilization of RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) for intelligent automation
- **Enterprise-Grade Quality**: Comprehensive testing, TypeScript safety, and production-ready reliability
- **Data Sovereignty**: Complete control over your customer data through self-hosting
- **Modern Technology**: Built on the latest Next.js stack for performance and developer experience

## The Problem

### Data Sovereignty & Cost Explosion

Traditional CRM platforms force businesses to choose between powerful features and data control. Proprietary solutions like Salesforce and HubSpot charge escalating subscription fees (often $50-150+ per user/month) while storing sensitive customer data on their servers. For businesses in regulated industries or privacy-conscious markets, this creates compliance risks and vendor dependency.

**Our Solution:** NextCRM provides enterprise-grade CRM capabilities that you host on your own infrastructure, ensuring complete data control while eliminating per-user subscription costs.

### Manual Data Entry & Information Overload

Sales teams waste 17% of their time on data entry and struggle to find relevant customer context when they need it. Traditional CRMs become data graveyards where valuable insights are buried in fields and notes.

**Our Solution:** AI-powered data enrichment automatically fills in lead information, while RAG-based retrieval surfaces relevant customer context exactly when needed—turning your CRM into an intelligent sales assistant.

### One-Size-Fits-All Limitations

Off-the-shelf CRMs force businesses to adapt their processes to rigid software constraints. Customization requires expensive consultants and proprietary programming languages (like Salesforce Apex).

**Our Solution:** Built on open-source Next.js with modern TypeScript, NextCRM is fully customizable by any web developer. Fork it, extend it, or contribute back to the community—you control the roadmap.

### AI Isolation

Most CRMs bolt-on AI features as afterthoughts, creating disconnected experiences. RAG technology exists but remains underutilized in customer relationship management.

**Our Solution:** NextCRM is architected AI-first, with RAG deeply integrated into lead generation, data enrichment, and sales analytics—making AI a natural part of daily workflows.

**Impact:** A 50-person team saves $30,000-75,000 annually compared to Salesforce or HubSpot Professional tiers.

## Key Features

### Core CRM Features

- **Comprehensive Contact Management**: Full-featured accounts, contacts, and leads management with customizable fields and relationship tracking
- **Opportunity Pipeline**: Visual pipeline management with customizable stages, forecasting, and deal tracking
- **Task & Activity Tracking**: Integrated task management with kanban boards, due dates, and team collaboration
- **Contract Management**: Store, organize, and track customer contracts with version control
- **Advanced Search**: Full-text search across all CRM entities for instant information retrieval

### AI-Powered Features

- **AI Lead Generation**: Automated lead discovery and qualification using RAG to identify high-potential prospects
- **Intelligent Data Enrichment**: Automatically enhance lead and contact records with AI-sourced information (company details, social profiles, industry data)
- **Sales AI Assistant**: Natural language interface to query your CRM data, get insights, and receive AI-driven recommendations
- **AI Invoice Processing**: Automated invoice parsing and data extraction using advanced OCR and ML models
- **Smart Notifications**: AI-generated daily summaries of tasks, opportunities, and priority actions

### Project Management Integration

- **Kanban Boards**: Visual project tracking with drag-and-drop task organization
- **Project-Customer Linking**: Connect projects directly to CRM accounts and opportunities
- **Team Collaboration**: Assign tasks, track progress, and coordinate across sales and delivery teams

### Document & Communication

- **Document Management**: Centralized file storage with organization, tagging, and access control
- **Integrated Email Client**: Built-in IMAP/SMTP email functionality to manage customer communications without leaving the CRM
- **Email Campaigns**: MailChimp and Listmonk integration for marketing automation (planned)
- **React Email Templates**: Beautiful, responsive email templates built with React

### Financial Management

- **Invoice Management**: Create, track, and manage customer invoices with AI-powered parsing
- **XML Export**: Generate standardized invoice formats for accounting system integration
- **Revenue Analytics**: Track opportunities, win rates, and revenue forecasts

### Analytics & Reporting

- **Interactive Dashboards**: Real-time charts and visualizations powered by Tremor
- **Custom Reports**: Build and save custom reports across all CRM data
- **Sales Analytics**: Pipeline analysis, conversion tracking, and performance metrics

### Enterprise Features

- **Multi-Tenant Architecture**: Support multiple organizations with data isolation
- **Role-Based Access Control**: Granular permissions for teams and users
- **Authentication Options**: Google, GitHub, and credential-based authentication via NextAuth.js
- **Internationalization**: Multi-language support via next-intl
- **API Access**: RESTful API for integrations and custom applications

### Developer Experience

- **Modern Tech Stack**: Next.js 15, React 19, TypeScript for best-in-class developer experience
- **Comprehensive Testing**: End-to-end tests (Cypress) and unit tests (Jest) for reliability
- **Type Safety**: Full TypeScript implementation with no 'any' types
- **Docker Support**: Containerized deployment for easy self-hosting
- **Open Source**: MIT license, community contributions welcome
