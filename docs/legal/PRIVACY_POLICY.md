## Introduction

Welcome to NextCRM, a Customer Relationship Management platform operated by NextCRM, Inc. ("NextCRM," "we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), Privacy Act 1988 (Australia), and Privacy Act 2020 (New Zealand).

**By using NextCRM, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.** If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use our services.

**Important:** This policy applies to data we collect and process. If you use NextCRM to manage your own customer data, you are the data controller for that information, and you are responsible for complying with applicable privacy laws in your own use of our platform.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide, maintain, and improve our services.

### 1.1 Account Information

When you register for a NextCRM account, we collect:

- **Identity Information:** Name, email address, company name, job title
- **Authentication Information:** Password (encrypted), OAuth tokens from third-party providers (Google, GitHub)
- **Organization Information:** Organization name, billing address, tax identification number (if applicable)
- **Profile Information:** Profile photo, time zone, language preferences

### 1.2 Billing Information

When you subscribe to a paid plan, we collect:

- **Payment Information:** Credit card details (processed and stored by Stripe, our PCI DSS Level 1 certified payment processor)
- **Billing Address:** Street address, city, state/province, postal code, country
- **Transaction History:** Invoices, payment receipts, subscription changes
- **Tax Information:** VAT numbers, ABN (Australia), NZBN (New Zealand) for tax compliance

**Important:** We do not store complete credit card numbers on our servers. Payment information is securely processed and stored by Stripe.

### 1.3 Customer Relationship Data

As a CRM platform, you use NextCRM to store and manage your own customer data. This may include:

- **Contact Information:** Names, email addresses, phone numbers, physical addresses
- **Company Information:** Company names, industry, revenue, employee count
- **Communication History:** Email content, call notes, meeting records
- **Transaction Data:** Invoices, quotes, contracts, payment terms
- **Custom Fields:** Any additional information you choose to store about your customers

**Note:** You are the data controller for this information. We act as a data processor, processing this data on your behalf according to your instructions.

### 1.4 Usage Information

We automatically collect information about how you use NextCRM:

- **Device Information:** Browser type, operating system, device type, screen resolution
- **IP Address:** Your IP address for security, fraud prevention, and approximate location
- **Log Data:** Timestamps, page views, features used, API calls, error logs
- **Session Information:** Login times, session duration, logout events
- **Performance Data:** Page load times, feature response times, error rates

### 1.5 Communication Data

We collect information when you communicate with us:

- **Support Requests:** Email correspondence, live chat messages, support tickets
- **Feedback:** Feature requests, bug reports, survey responses
- **Marketing Communications:** Newsletter subscriptions, event registrations, webinar attendance

### 1.6 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

- **Authentication:** Maintain your login session
- **Security:** Protect against CSRF attacks and unauthorized access
- **Analytics:** Understand how users interact with our platform (using first-party analytics only)
- **Preferences:** Remember your settings and preferences

See our [Cookie Policy](#) for detailed information about the cookies we use.

## 3. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, we process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

### 3.1 Contract Performance (GDPR Article 6(1)(b))

We process data necessary to:
- Provide NextCRM services you've subscribed to
- Process payments and manage your subscription
- Respond to support requests and fulfill contractual obligations

### 3.2 Legal Obligation (GDPR Article 6(1)(c))

We process data to comply with:
- Tax laws requiring retention of financial records (7 years)

- Court orders and lawful requests from authorities

### 3.3 Legitimate Interests (GDPR Article 6(1)(f))

We process data for our legitimate business interests, including:
- Fraud prevention and security monitoring
- Service improvement and product development
- Business analytics and operational optimization

We ensure these interests do not override your fundamental rights and freedoms.

### 3.4 Consent (GDPR Article 6(1)(a))

We obtain your explicit consent for:
- Marketing communications (newsletters, promotional emails)
- AI-powered features that process your data
- Email tracking and link click analytics (optional feature)
- Sharing data with third parties beyond service delivery

**You can withdraw consent at any time** by contacting privacy@nextcrm.io or using the unsubscribe link in marketing emails.

## 4. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations.

### 4.1 Active Accounts

- **Account Data:** Retained while your account is active
- **Customer Data:** Retained while your organization subscription is active
- **Usage Logs:** Retained for 90 days for security and troubleshooting
- **Audit Logs:** Retained for 90 days (or longer for compliance investigations)

### 4.2 Deleted Accounts

- **Grace Period:** 30-day soft delete period (account can be restored)
- **Permanent Deletion:** After 30 days, all data is permanently deleted
- **Backup Retention:** Data in backups is automatically purged after 7 days
- **Exception:** Financial records retained for 7 years for tax compliance

### 4.3 Financial and Tax Records

- **Invoices:** Retained for 7 years (legal requirement)
- **Payment Records:** Retained for 7 years (legal requirement)
- **Tax Documents:** Retained for 7 years (legal requirement)

### 4.4 Anonymized Data

- **Aggregated Analytics:** Retained indefinitely (no personal identifiers)
- **Product Insights:** Anonymized usage patterns for product improvement

## 5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

### 5.1 Right of Access (GDPR Article 15)

You have the right to:
- Request a copy of your personal data we hold
- Understand how we process your data
- Receive data in a machine-readable format (JSON export)

**How to exercise:** Contact privacy@nextcrm.io or use the "Export Data" feature in your account settings (Settings > Privacy > Export Data).

### 5.2 Right to Rectification (GDPR Article 16)

You have the right to:
- Correct inaccurate personal data
- Complete incomplete personal data
- Update outdated information

**How to exercise:** Update your information directly in your account settings, or contact privacy@nextcrm.io for assistance.

### 5.3 Right to Erasure / "Right to be Forgotten" (GDPR Article 17)

You have the right to:
- Request deletion of your personal data
- Request deletion of your organization data
- Remove data that is no longer necessary

**How to exercise:** Delete your account in Settings > Account > Delete Organization, or contact privacy@nextcrm.io. Note: Financial records are retained for 7 years due to legal requirements.

### 5.4 Right to Data Portability (GDPR Article 20)

You have the right to:
- Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
- Transfer your data to another service provider

**How to exercise:** Use the "Export Data" feature (Settings > Privacy > Export Data) to download your data as JSON.

### 5.5 Right to Object (GDPR Article 21)

You have the right to:
- Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- Object to direct marketing at any time
- Object to automated decision-making

**How to exercise:** Contact privacy@nextcrm.io or use the unsubscribe link in marketing emails.

### 5.6 Right to Restrict Processing (GDPR Article 18)

You have the right to:
- Restrict processing while we verify data accuracy
- Restrict processing instead of deletion
- Restrict processing while objection is pending

**How to exercise:** Contact privacy@nextcrm.io with your request.

### 5.7 Response Time

We will respond to your rights requests within:
- **30 days** for standard requests
- **45 days** for complex requests (with notification)

## 8. Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your data.

### 8.1 Encryption

- **In Transit:** TLS 1.3 encryption for all data transmitted over the internet
- **At Rest:** AES-256 encryption for all data stored in MongoDB Atlas
- **Passwords:** bcrypt hashing with 10 rounds (industry standard)
- **API Keys:** Stored as encrypted environment variables

### 8.2 Access Controls

- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC):** 4-tier permission system (OWNER, ADMIN, MEMBER, VIEWER)
- **Multi-Tenancy Isolation:** Organization data strictly separated
- **Authentication:** NextAuth.js with JWT tokens (30-day expiry)
- **Session Management:** httpOnly cookies, secure flag, SameSite protection

### 8.3 Network Security

- **DDoS Protection:** Cloudflare and Vercel DDoS mitigation
- **Rate Limiting:** Plan-based API rate limits to prevent abuse
- **IP Filtering:** Optional IP whitelist for Enterprise customers
- **CORS:** Strict cross-origin resource sharing policies

### 8.4 Operational Security

- **Audit Logging:** All data access and modifications logged (90-day retention)
- **Monitoring:** 24/7 automated monitoring for security incidents
- **Backups:** Automated daily backups with 7-day retention
- **Incident Response:** Documented incident response procedures
- **Vulnerability Scanning:** Automated dependency scanning (Dependabot)

### 8.5 Compliance Certifications

- **SOC 2 Type II:** In progress (target Q2 2026)
- **GDPR:** Self-assessed as compliant (ongoing)
- **PCI DSS:** Level 1 (via Stripe for payment processing)

### 8.6 No Absolute Security

**Important:** While we implement industry-standard security measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security against all threats. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your account credentials.
